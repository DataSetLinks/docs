#### **`select`**

* **Description**: Specifies the fields or attributes to include in the query result, narrowing the output to only the fields of interest.

* **Syntax**: `select("field1", "field2", ...)`

* **Example**:
  `OntologyObject("Movies").select("name", "year").get()`
